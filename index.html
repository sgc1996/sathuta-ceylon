About Us

SATHUTA CEYLON EXPORT

Supporting sustainable agriculture in Sri Lanka by using a clever-mix of inorganic and organic fertilizers to bring the maximum healthy yield is what brings happiness to our team of committed agro-experts.

Food Security is prescribed by FAO is at the heart of all country leaders in these climate-sensitive time and we hope to make their lives easy by continuously investing in R&D to meet the country food security goals whilst making the “excess crop” reach overseas markets through our trading arm sathuta Ceylon exports.

We know our soil better than anyone and moving from traditional farming practices to modern agricultural norms is a skill we develop daily to battle adverse climate conditions affecting food crops. Globally growing population, less ground to plant food crops, escalating cost of labor, fertilizers, water, pesticides and weedicides are the challenges confronting global farming communities on a day to day basis and sathuta is fully aware of these obstacles.

That’s why we use the inorganic and organic mix to retain more nutrients in the topsoil for longer periods of time.
